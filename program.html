Social Dinner venue

The social dinner will take place on Thursday 14th at 20:00 at the Restaurant "Al Bersagliere", located in Via Dietro Pallone, 1.

The guided tour of the city will take place on Thursday 14th at 18:00, right before the social dinner. The gathering point is set at the "Statua di Vittorio Emanuele II" and it's recommended to show up 10 minutes earlier to not miss the beginning of the tour.
