Accomodation

Verona offers a wide range of accommodation options to suit different preferences and budgets.

When booking accommodation in Verona, it's advisable to consider the proximity to the school venue, and preferably near transportation hubs. This will ensure convenience and easy access to everything Verona has to offer.
