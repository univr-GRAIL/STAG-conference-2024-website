Welcome to Verona!

Smart Tools and Applications in Graphics (STAG) is an annual international conference organized by the Italian Chapter of the Eurographics association.

Call for papers

STAG 2024 is organized by University of Verona and EGit Association.

The objective of the conference is to spread knowledge and innovative concepts regarding the theoretical and practical aspects of Computer Graphics.

It aims to unite researchers and professionals from national and international scientific communities, allowing them to exchange their latest advancements.

The conference welcomes submissions that propose fresh approaches to address practical issues, intelligent solutions to enhance existing techniques and algorithms for real-world use, as well as system and workflow papers that demonstrate their impact on practical applications.
We extend an invitation for contributions in the form of full papers posters, as detailed on the call for papers page.
